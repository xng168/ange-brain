## What I can say now (all inference, not your data)

**The math on your goal.** 7,009 → 15,000 by Dec 31 = ~8,000 net new followers in ~5.5 months, ~330/week. At a typical 1–2% view-to-follow conversion for accounts your size, that's roughly 15,000–35,000 views/week hitting *non-followers*. That's very achievable, but not at a casual posting cadence — it usually requires 4–7 posts/week with at least 1–2 modest outliers (50k+ views) per month doing most of the work. Growth at this size is spiky, not linear.

**Where sub-10k accounts almost always lose viewers** (verify against your retention graphs):
- **0–3 sec:** hook states the topic instead of the tension. "Today I'm going to show you..." dies; a visual or verbal open loop survives.
- **3–8 sec:** the "second hook" gap — intro rambles before payoff starts. This is the single most common kill zone.
- **Last 20%:** outro/CTA padding tanks completion rate. Cut the goodbye; end abruptly on the payoff.

**Generic growth levers, ranked by impact-to-effort** (I'll re-rank once I see your data):
1. **Rewrite hooks on your existing best-performing topics** — highest ROI, near-zero effort. Re-make your top video with a stronger first 2 seconds; proven topic + better hook beats new ideas.
2. **Cut video length until completion rate exceeds ~40–50%** (short-form) — completion is the heaviest-weighted signal at your account size.
3. **Consistency floor of 4–5 posts/week for 30 days** — the algorithm needs volume to find your outliers.
4. **TikTok SEO** — searchable phrasing in caption, on-screen text, and spoken audio. Search traffic is a growing share of views in 2025–26 and compounds, unlike FYP spikes.
5. **Reply-to-comment videos on anything that gets traction** — cheap way to chain a video's momentum.

**30-day plan skeleton** (I'll make it specific once I know your niche): weeks 1–2, post 5x/week testing 3 content pillars × 2 hook styles each; kill any pillar averaging below your account's median views after 4 attempts. Success thresholds to watch: avg watch time ≥ 50% of video length, completion ≥ 40%, FYP share of traffic ≥ 70%, and ≥ 0.8 follows per 1,000 views. Weeks 3–4: double down on the winning pillar, remake your two best performers with new hooks.
