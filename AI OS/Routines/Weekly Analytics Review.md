# Weekly Analytics Review

A short weekly routine to keep a pulse on Ange.beee's growth.

## Checklist (~15 min, once a week)
1. Open **Metricool** dashboard — check scheduled vs. posted content, overall reach/engagement trend
2. Open **TikTok Studio** (in-app) — check top-performing videos, audience retention, traffic source breakdown
3. Open **Instagram Professional Dashboard** — check reach, saves, shares, follower growth
4. Note anything surprising (a post that overperformed/underperformed, a follower spike) in [[Content/Analytics]]
5. Adjust next week's content plan based on what worked
