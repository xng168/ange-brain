# Productivity Self-Care Pet Venture Spec & Platform Validation

- **Venture established:** Created full write-up for roaming screen-pet product (pet reacts to your screen time, ambient presence + self-care nudge). Registered in Ventures index, decision block added to Open Items, logged to history.

- **Distinctiveness validated:** Refined concept escapes two crowded categories (Finch-like check-ins, app blockers) and fills proven-but-unowned niche: roaming desktop companions (Shimeji, Desktop Goose, eSheep) have no polished, monetisable, self-care-branded version. **Moat is character design & audience, not tech.** Generic screen-time tracking (no app-specific alerts) is simpler engineering.

- **Platform recommendation reversed:** Desktop-first (Electron/Tauri) is correct—not iOS. iOS forbids roaming overlays and drawing over other apps; the hero mechanic only works on Windows/macOS (transparent always-on-top window) or Android (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW). Desktop is both easiest to build *and* truest to vision; real work is sprite animation & personality, not plumbing.

- **Key tradeoff:** Desktop app is different stack from Expo/React Native momentum on Mandarin Kids App—not a dealbreaker, just honest tradeoff on resource timing.
