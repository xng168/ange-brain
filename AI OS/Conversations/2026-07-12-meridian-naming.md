# Meridian App Naming Strategy

## What was done
- Generated and evaluated brand names for an astrology × personality app (Western astrology + Chinese BaZi + MBTI)
- Analyzed "Meridian" against competitor positioning (INNI, Co-Star, Dimensional)
- Validated "Meridian" as a technical bridge between two distinct traditions

## Key decisions
- **Selected name: Meridian** — dual meaning strengthens the brand
  - Western astrology: MC-IC axis (Midheaven to Imum Coeli) connecting public self ↔ private self
  - Chinese tradition: Energy meridians (jīngluò) channeling qi through the body
- **Brand narrative**: "Meridian — the line where your public self meets your private self, where East meets West, where the sky meets your energy"
- **Caveat**: Name is fairly crowded already (Meridian Energy, Health, CMS, audio brands); requires trademark/domain check
