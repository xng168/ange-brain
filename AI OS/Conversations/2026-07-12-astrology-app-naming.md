# Astrology App Brand Name Brainstorm

## Summary
Generated comprehensive brand name suggestions for the astrology app (Western + BaZi + personality type positioning), grouped by strategy and including rationale for each.

## Key Outcomes
- **Top recommendation: Meridian** — encodes the app's core differentiator (East-meets-West synthesis) directly into the name; reads as modern, one-word-brandable, and aligns with competitor aesthetic (INNI, Co-Star, Dimensional)
- **Strong alternative: Yuán (缘)** — signals the Chinese cultural angle immediately; means "fated connection/destiny"; more distinctive and directly resonates with target audience (Ange.beee demographic)
- **Full shortlist organized by angle:**
  - East-meets-West bridge: Meridian, Pillars (Four Pillars of Destiny), Yuán
  - Abstract/coined (INNI-style): Vela, Aven, Sola, Mira
  - Self-knowledge / warm tone (CHANI-style): Kin, Kindred, Vera
