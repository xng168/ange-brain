# TCM App: Data Sourcing & Authenticity Framework

- **Created TCM App venture note** with market research under `Ventures/` and linked it from the index
- **Mapped credible TCM data hierarchy**: classical public-domain texts (Compendium of Materia Medica, Huangdi Neijing as CC-BY-SA foundation) → modern secondary references (Paul Pitchford model) → academic pharmacology databases (TCMSP, ETCM, TCMID for cross-validation) → statistical nutrient correlation for novel foods
- **Defined data schema** with thermal nature, five flavors, organ affinity, actions/indications, contraindications, source citations, and confidence/consensus flags to show work transparently
- **Key decision: mandatory human review layer** — require 2–3 licensed TCM practitioners to validate and achieve consensus before shipping (handles disagreements, provides liability cover, enables marketing claim)
- **Statistical bridge for modern foods** — peer-reviewed methodology uses nutrient composition (water, potassium/sodium ratio) to infer thermal nature for foods with no classical precedent, then confirm with human review
