# TCM App: MVP Pivot from Tracker to Ingredient Reference

- **Pivoted MVP scope** from MyFitnessPal-style daily tracker to lightweight searchable ingredient-reference tool (ginger → TCM properties) — lower complexity, faster validation, maps directly to TikTok content test
- **Identified critical moat gap:** free web competitors (tcmfoodtherapy.net, clinic blogs) already do ingredient lookup; differentiation needs personalization (constitution quiz badges), practitioner-verified trust branding, and/or symptom-based search, not just being first
- **Monetization rethink:** reference apps monetize via ads/one-time unlock/affiliate links, not subscriptions — need to revisit pricing hypothesis beyond MyFitnessPal assumption
- **Documented 9 open gaps** (symptom-correlation pseudoscience risk, AI boundary rules, regulatory/legal, practitioner involvement, retention mechanics, entity setup) — saved to TCM App/index.md to seed Super Prompt
- **Proposed product ladder:** free ingredient lookup (acquisition) → personalized recommendations via constitution quiz (retention) → paid tracking/journal layer (monetization, once trust earned)
- **Next decision:** nail differentiation angle (personalization vs. free content) before rewriting venture note, or lock in MVP scope now and rewrite around the lighter scope?
