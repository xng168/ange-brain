# Social Media Management Tools

Researched 2026-07-12 for Ange.beee (TikTok + Instagram creator work). Compared on free-tier limits, TikTok/Instagram support (incl. auto-publish), analytics depth, and content calendar quality.

## Comparison

| Tool | Free tier | TikTok + IG support | Analytics on free tier | Content calendar |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Metricool** | $0/mo forever — 1 brand, ~50 scheduled posts/mo, 30-day analytics history, AI copy assistant included | Both included, plus FB/YouTube/Pinterest/Google Business | Yes — 30-day rolling history | Strong, purpose-built calendar view |
| **Buffer** | $0/mo forever — 3 channels connected, up to 10 queued posts per channel | Both included; auto-publish works for Business/Creator accounts (images, carousels, Stories, video) | Minimal — deeper reporting is a paid "Analyze" add-on | Simple, clean queue-style calendar |
| **Later** | **No free plan as of 2026** — 14-day trial only, then from ~$18.75/mo (annual) | Both included on paid plans | Included on paid plans | Very strong, visual-first calendar (its signature feature) |
| **Planable** | $0/mo forever — unlimited workspaces/users, but 50 posts **lifetime** (doesn't reset monthly) | Both included for scheduling | **No** — analytics is a paid add-on ($14/workspace/mo) | Excellent for collaboration/approvals, less analytics-focused |

## Recommendation: start with Metricool (free tier)

Metricool is the only option that gives analytics, TikTok + Instagram scheduling, and a real calendar all for $0 — no lifetime post cap (unlike Planable) and no vanished free plan (unlike Later). Buffer is a reasonable backup if channel-queue style scheduling is preferred, but its free tier has no meaningful analytics.

## Native analytics worth checking directly (in addition to Metricool)
- **TikTok Studio** (in-app) — video-level performance, audience retention, traffic sources; not replicated in third-party tools.
- **Instagram Professional Dashboard** — reach, saves, shares, follower demographics; also not fully replicated elsewhere.

Metricool is a good scheduling/calendar layer, but these two native dashboards remain the most accurate source for platform-specific metrics — worth a manual look weekly (see [[AI OS/Routines/Weekly Analytics Review]]).
