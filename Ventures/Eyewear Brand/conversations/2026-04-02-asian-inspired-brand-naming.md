Here are some evocative word directions that tie to Chinese/Asian cultural heritage while feeling modern and wearable as a fashion brand:

**Luck & Prosperity**
- **Fortune** *(your pick — classic)*
- **Jade** — the most revered stone in Chinese culture, symbolising purity and grace
- **Gilded** — gold is deeply tied to luck and prosperity
- **Ingot** — ancient gold/silver currency, a symbol of wealth

**Nature & Elements**
- **Lotus** — purity and resilience, an iconic motif in Chinese art
- **Crane** — longevity and elegance, a beloved symbol
- **Ember** — fire is one of the five elements (五行)
- **Mist** — evokes Chinese landscape painting
- **Bloom** — florals are central to Chinese textile and art tradition

**Sky & Cosmos**
- **Lunar** — the lunar calendar governs Chinese festivals and culture
- **Solstice** — seasonal festivals are sacred in Chinese heritage
- **Celestial** — the Emperor was "Son of Heaven"; very tied to Chinese imperial culture
- **Dynasty** — direct, bold, and instantly recognisable

**Materials & Craft**
- **Lacquer** — the ancient Chinese art form, sleek and high-fashion sounding
- **Silk** — China gave silk to the world; timeless and luxurious
- **Porcelain** — synonymous with Chinese craft and delicacy
- **Vermilion** — the iconic red of Chinese culture, also a pigment

**Directional picks for a fashion brand:**
| Vibe | Name |
|---|---|
| Luxury / minimal | **Jade**, **Lacquer**, **Porcelain** |
| Bold / statement | **Dynasty**, **Celestial**, **Vermilion** |
| Soft / romantic | **Lotus**, **Lunar**, **Bloom** |
| Streetwear / modern | **Ember**, **Gilded**, **Crane** |

Here are short, punchy words tied to prosperity and Chinese cultural symbolism:

**One syllable — maximum impact**
- **Fù** (福) — the most iconic Chinese character, literally means fortune/prosperity
- **Jin** — gold in Chinese (金), clean and modern
- **Bao** — precious/treasure (寶)
- **Wei** — greatness/power
- **Lung** — dragon, the ultimate symbol of imperial power and luck
- **Chai** — wealth/firewood, but feels very current
- **Yù** — jade, also means abundance

**Two syllables — still sharp**

- **Fengshui** — balance and flow, universally recognised
- **Wulong** — dark dragon, very cool for a fashion brand
- **Baofu** — precious fortune
- **Jinfu** — gold prosperity

**Pure English words, short and prosperity-linked**

- **Crest** — peak/top, implies elevation
- **Bloom** — flourishing
- **Reign** — power and dominance
