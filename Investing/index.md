# Investing

Learning share investing and trading across international markets: Australia (ASX), Taiwan (TWSE), Hong Kong (HKEX), and the United States.

**Scope:** education, analysis, tracking, and news — not automated trade execution. This is a research/decision-support system, not a trading bot.

## Subfolders
- [[Investing/Education]] — fundamentals, market mechanics, analysis frameworks
- [[Investing/Markets]] — per-market reference notes (ASX, TWSE, HKEX, US)
- [[Investing/Watchlist]] — stocks being tracked, one note per stock with your thesis
- [[Investing/Journal]] — decision journal — what you decided, why, and the outcome

## Tools
- Daily market briefing via Telegram at 8am (see [[AI OS/Routines/Market Briefing]])
- On-demand: ask about any stock/market anytime
- Price/ratio lookups via `_sources/market_data.py` (free Yahoo Finance data)
